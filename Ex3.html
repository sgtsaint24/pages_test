The 3D0X4 transition

Previously, the 3D0 and 3D1 career fields were used to describe Air Force members with Cyber Space operations careers, as shown in in table 1. These AFSCs (Air Force Specialty Codes) are now present in the United States Air Force under the 1D7X1 career field, as seen in Table 2.

Table 1

AFSC Identifier AFSC Title
3D0X1 Knowledge Management
3D0X2 Cyber Systems Operations
3D0X3 Cyber Surety
3D0X4 Computer Systems Programming

3D1X2 Cyber Transport Systems
3D1X3 Radio Frequency Transmission Systems
3D1X4 Spectrum Operations
3D1X5 Ground Radar Systems
